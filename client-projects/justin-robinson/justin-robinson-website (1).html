The Process

How It Works.

01
Book a Free Discovery Call

A quick 15-minute conversation. Tell Justin your goals, your history, and what you've already tried. No pressure — just clarity on whether you're a good fit.

02
Get Your Custom Plan

Within 48 hours of joining, Justin builds your program from scratch — a tailored workout schedule and, if you add meals, a personalized weekly menu built around your macros and dietary preferences.

03
Show Up & Execute

Train in person, eat your prepared meals, and check in weekly so Justin can track your progress and adjust the plan. Your only job is to show up — everything else is handled.

04
Track, Adjust, Repeat

Weekly check-ins keep the program evolving with your body. Hitting a plateau? Justin adjusts. Ahead of schedule? Justin pushes harder. The plan never gets stale.
