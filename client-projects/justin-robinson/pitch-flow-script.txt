Justin Mockup Demo Flow

1) Website hook
- Start at hero and value proposition.
- Point out the CTA hierarchy (Book a Free Call + secondary Explore Services).
- Mention this is optimized for visitor to booked call.

2) Social proof and system clarity
- Scroll to Results + Testimonials.
- Explain this section is structured to prove outcomes first.
- Transition into How It Works to show process clarity.

3) Conversion section
- Open Book Your Strategy Call.
- Show the built-in scheduler fallback UI and selected slot state chips.
- Highlight this still presents cleanly even when embed availability changes.

4) Lead capture path
- Show Lead Capture form and Contact section.
- Explain lower-intent visitors are captured without breaking flow.

5) Admin CRM handoff
- Navigate to /app.
- Show dashboard KPIs and activity feed to explain operational visibility.

6) Lead management
- Navigate to /app/leads.
- Show pipeline view and lead table.
- Open a lead profile, then open Convert to Client modal.

7) Client operations
- Navigate to /app/clients and open a client profile.
- Show intake context, assignment placeholders, and timeline.

8) Fulfillment modules
- Show /app/workouts and /app/meals.
- Explain how program assignment and meal operations are represented.

9) Revenue + reporting
- Show /app/billing and /app/analytics.
- Highlight failed payment alert, invoice view, and funnel/revenue visuals.

10) Integrations and onboarding visibility
- Show /app/settings and /app/onboarding.
- Close with: website lead -> booking -> conversion -> onboarding -> ongoing client management.
